Overview

The Hardware module, and its classes, provide information about the hardware on which it is being run. This is used internally by Homebrew, and can also be used in formulae.

Hardware and its classes are extended with platform-specific modules, such as MacCPUs.

def self.cores_as_words
  case Hardware.processor_count
  when 1 then 'single'
  when 2 then 'dual'
  when 4 then 'quad'
  else
    Hardware.processor_count
  end
end
